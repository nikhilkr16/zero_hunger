SOLVING WORLD HUNGER

REASON BEHIND FOOD Shortage

CLIMATE CHANGE

Climate change disrupts weather patterns, leading to extreme events like droughts and floods, impacting crop yields and causing food shortages, ultimately contributing to world hunger.

WAR

Wars cause food shortage in a country by disrupting agriculture and trade routes

POVERTY

Poverty limits people's access to food, contributing to widespread hunger worldwide.

GOVERNMENT SCHEMES

Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana (PMGKAY)

Provides 5 kg of free food grains per month to over 80 crore beneficiaries.

National Food Security Act (NFSA)

Covers up to 75% of the rural population and 50% of the urban population. Provides subsidized food grains to eligible households through ration shops

Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY)

Targets the poorest of the poor, providing 35 kg of food grains per month at highly subsidized rates
